Bachelor of Arts/Film from the University of Winnipeg

Ever since I got my hands on a camera when I was just 7 years old, I was using it to tell stories. I made my first film when I was 12 with nothing more than a laptop webcam, a soundboard on my iPad, and my clarinet. Creative freedom is something I value greatly and I've always appreciated my passion for filmmaking. I hope to share my stories and passion with the rest of the world.
